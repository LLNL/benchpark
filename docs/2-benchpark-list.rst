==============
Benchpark List
==============

To list all benchmarks and systems available in Benchpark::

    cd bin
    benchpark list

Once you have decided on a ``system`` you will use, and the ``benchmark/ProgrammingModel``
to run, you can proceed to :doc:`4-benchpark-setup`.
